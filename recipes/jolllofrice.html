Jollof rice recipe

Description

This is not just any rice, This is Naija Jollof as some will call. It is mostly eaten on special occasions like wedding party and the likes. Because of it's special taste and color, it'll beat your expectations. Let's dive into the Recipe

Ingredients For the chicken

  • Washed Rice
  • 2 pounds chicken drumsticks
  • ½ large onion, diced
  • 1 (2 inch) piece fresh ginger root, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 cubes chicken bouillon, crushed
  • 2 cloves garlic, diced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • Chicken flavor spices
  • 1 cup water

Ingredients For the Rice

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ½ large onion, diced
  • 1 (14 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 (10 ounce) mixed vegetables (carrots, corn, peas)
  • Jollof Rice seasonings to taste and color
  • Fresh grounded tomato (Optional)
  • 3 cups parboiled rice

Steps:

  1. Place chicken drumsticks or any protein of your choice in a large Dutch oven over medium heat. Mix well with the spices listed. Pour in water, mix, cover the pot, and bring to a gentle simmer; cook for 15 minutes. Remove from heat.
  2. Transfer chicken to a baking dish using a slotted spoon. Strain cooking liquid through a fine-mesh sieve. Reserve 1 1/2 cups liquid. Discard solids.
  3. Bake chicken in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the middle and the juices run clear, about 30 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the thickest part should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  4. Heat 3 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large pot over medium-low heat and cook 1/2 onion until soft and translucent but not browned, about 5 minutes. Add tomato sauce; cook and stir until slightly thickened and infused into the oil, 5 to 7 minutes
  5. Stir reserved chicken broth, salt, and pepper into the pot. Bring to a simmer; add rice. Cook, stirring often, until rice is almost tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Continue cooking until rice is tender and creamy, about 5 minutes.

Serve with a chilled drink and enjoy
